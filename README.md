# Where does the smart reside?

**A systematic mapping study of the locus of intelligence in smart manufacturing research.**

Working thesis: smart manufacturing research systematically locates intelligence in the
manufacturing process and its resources — machines, scheduling systems, factories, and lately the
human worker (the *processor*) — rather than in the product being made (the *processee*), its
lifecycle, and the outcomes it delivers to users. This repository contains the frozen review
protocol, its decision history, pilot studies, and supporting material.
